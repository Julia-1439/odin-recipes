Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Home

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a staple American food. It is often eaten as a snack or a light meal. The choice of bread, peanut butter, and jam make for many varieties to eat them in to keep it interesting! I personally enjoy using wheat bread for a fiber boost, light jam to keep the sugar low, and to eat it as a snack any time of day.

Ingredients

  • choice of bread (wheat is the superior option)
  • choice of peanut butter (e.g. smooth, chunky)
  • choice of jam (e.g. grape, strawberry

Steps

  1. Put two slices of bread side by side
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread jam on the other slice
  4. Put the two slices together
  5. (Optional) Sear it or toast it
  6. Enjoy!
