Tonkatsu Ramen

Home

Description

The tonkatsu ramen is a staple of Japanese cuisine. It features noodles in a savory pork bone broth served with a wide choice of ingredients, including eggs, chashu, seaweed, and spring onions. It is one of my all-time favorite dishes.

Ingredients

    This is a list of ingredients you need to make tonkatsu ramen!
  • pork bones
  • pork
  • ramen noodles
  • Japanese spices
  • egg
  • spring onions

Steps

  1. Create the tonkatsu broth (12 hours)
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Sear the chashu pork
  4. Slice spring onions
  5. Soft-boil the egg
  6. Assemble the bowl
  7. Enjoy!
